This wiki brings together the tools, guidance, and standards that support Ember’s work to accelerate the clean energy transition. It is designed to help you turn data into clear, credible, and impactful insights that inform decision-makers and shape public debate.

Our approach is grounded in strong evidence and clear communication. We prioritise simplicity, consistency, and accessibility so that our analysis can reach the widest possible audience and drive real-world change.

Use this space to navigate how we work, develop your skills, and produce high-quality outputs that contribute to Ember’s mission.
